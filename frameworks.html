Class

These fish are a part of the shark class! Known as chondrithyes, and containing all sharks, rays, and skates, it's a pretty cool class to be a part of.

Size

Females can get up to 4ft! While males only get up to 3.5ft, just like me fr (short kings)

Cute

They are cutie patooties! With their large eyes and smallness, these little guys fit their name

Muddy Fellers

While they are called Dogfish, they are also referred to as Mud Sharks

Vulnerability?

They are currently not endangered which is great!

Species

Their scientific name is Squalus acanthias
