About us

Reisons is a second location of The Ice Cream Shack of Sunnyland, which serves the East Peoria and Washington community! We have soft serve ice cream in vanilla, chocolate, and a swirl of the two— as well as our "Wizards" which are made by blending ice cream together with toppings (such as Peanut Butter Cups)!
